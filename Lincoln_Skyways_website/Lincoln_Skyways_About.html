Lincoln Skyways opened its doors in September of 1985. For 30 years, we have developed a reputation of honest, top-quality workmanship. Since our seasoned team has over 100 plus years of combined experience, you know you can count on us to get the job done right. Our integrity and professionalism has built a business known for our attention to detail. We take time with our customers to explain any needed repairs in a simple concise fashion. Good communication is one of our trademarks. At Lincoln Skyways, we have built a reputation by being a fair shop that proudly stands behind our work. We work on most makes and models of aircraft, and we specialize in maintaining high performance twin and single engine aircraft. We are also a Cessna Service Center
